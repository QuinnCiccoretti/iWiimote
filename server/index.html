iWiimote Getting Started Guide

Instructions for iOS

Click first on the myCA.cer link below. Go to your Settings->General->Profiles, and trust the 'iWiimote CA'. This is iWiimote's special certificate authority. Next go to Settings->General->About->Certificate Trust Settings and give iWiimote CA root privledges. This will allow iWiimote to act as a certificate authority on your phone, similarly to how UVA allows its students onto their local network or wifi.

Certificate Authority Profile

Next click on the iwiimote_server.cer below. Now trust the Profile of the IP address that was outputted in your console.
